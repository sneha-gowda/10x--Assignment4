Winter
winter generally begins on December 21 or 22.This is the winter solstice, the day of the year with the shortest period of daylight
Summer
Summer begins on June 20 or 21, the summer solstice, which has the most daylight of any day in the year.
Spring
Spring and fall, or autumn, begin on equinoxes, days that have equal amounts of daylight and darkness.
Fall
The vernal, or spring, equinox falls on March 20 or 21, and the autumnal equinox is on September 22 or 23.
